Broken-rung detection

PSA 7 Price Audit

Find cards whose PSA 7 price is implausibly low relative to raw, PSA 8–10, and clean cards with the same set and treatment.

Conservative by design: Raw above PSA 7 is not enough to trigger a flag. PSA 8 must confirm the inversion, the PSA 8–10 ladder must remain coherent, and at least eight monotonic cards from the same set and treatment (for example, Reverse Holo) must support it. The suggestion uses the lower quartile of those peer curves and is capped at 90% of PSA 8. Nothing changes until you select cards and accept.
